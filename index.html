Cherry

Coffee cherries, the vibrant and flavorful fruit of the coffee plant, are the heart of our business. Sourced from the world's most renowned coffee-growing regions, these cherries are meticulously harvested at peak ripeness to ensure exceptional quality. Our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing practices ensures that each coffee cherry is cultivated with care, respecting both the environment and the livelihoods of the farmers who nurture them.

Parchment

Coffee parchment, an essential component in the coffee production process, plays a pivotal role in maintaining the quality and integrity of the beans. Sourced from carefully selected coffee cherries, parchment serves as the protective layer surrounding the coffee bean during its drying phase. Its meticulous removal requires expertise to ensure the preservation of the bean's intrinsic flavors and characteristics.
